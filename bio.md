I am currently in my fifth year of pursuing a Ph.D. at the University of Michigan, working under the guidance of Professor Necmiye Ozay. Prior to this, I earned a B.S. degree in Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2016, followed by an M.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan in 2018. Outside of my academic pursuits, I find joy in playing badminton, exploring hiking trails, engaging in photography, and experimenting with various cooking techniques.
